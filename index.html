Testimonial

Bhawana joshi

Bhaiyo aur behno, aaj main aapko ek aise kalakar ke baare mein bataungi jo Aipan art mein mahir hain, unka naam hai Hema binwal. Maine unke kala ka karishma khud dekha hai, aur mujhe unki kala, mahirata aur iss parampara se judi khoobsurati ne sach mein prabhavit kiya.

Priya

Apki (Hema binwal) Aipan kala waakai anokhi hai.Apki rachnayein prabal rango, uljhe huye patternon aur sanskritik mahatva ke saath ek ek hai. Har kriti ek alag kahani sunati hai aur Uttarakhand, Bharat ki jahan Aipan kala ki mool sthal hai, ki gehraiyon ki paramparaon ko darust karti hai. Apki unmein se kalpana aur apani kala ko bachane aur badhane ki samarpan bhari koshish se mera mann chhua.
